E7018 — Mechanical & Physical Properties

Extra low hydrogen potassium coating — ~110% efficiency with iron powder

Classification AWS E7018 / E7018-1
Coating Type Extra low hydrogen potassium
Polarity DC or AC ≥ 70V
Welding Position All positions
AWS Standard A5.1 E7018-1 / E7018
ISO Standard ISO 2560-B-E 49 18-1A
GB/T Standard 5117 E5018-1 / E5018
Other Standards CSA W48 E4918-1-H8  ·  JIS Z3212 D5016
Baking required: 400°C for 1–2 hours before use. Electrodes ≤2.5 mm: 350°C, hold 1 hour.
